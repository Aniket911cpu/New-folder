Frequently Asked Questions

Can I capture images larger than my screen?

Yes! SnapFlow automatically scrolls through the page and stitches multiple screenshots together.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All captures are processed locally in your browser and stored in your Chrome profile. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Can I edit my captures?

Yes! The result page provides tools for drawing, highlighting, and basic editing.
